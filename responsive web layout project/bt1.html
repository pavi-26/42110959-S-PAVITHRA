PARIS

Paris, It is known as the most romantic city in the world, and is home to some world famous sights that are constantly shown in travel magazines, movies, and other works of art. Paris, the capital of France,
has a population of over two million people and is one of Europe's most-visited cities.

Since the 17th century, Paris has been one of the world's major centres of finance, diplomacy, commerce, culture, fashion, and gastronomy. For its leading role in the arts and sciences, as well as its early and extensive system of street lighting, in the 19th century, it became known as the City of Light. Paris is the capital of the country of France. It has long been one of western Europe's major centers of culture and business. Some of the world's greatest artists, writers, scholars, fashion designers, and chefs have lived in Paris. The city is also famous for its beauty.
